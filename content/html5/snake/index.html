Objective:

  • The objective of the game is to Score 5 points before the other snake.
  • The game is over if either one of the snakes get killed or scores 5 points.


Logic Design and Implementation:

  • Each eatable gives a special power to the snake.
  • The snake looses if it touches the border or the other snake's tail.
